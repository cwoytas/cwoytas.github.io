Our capstone project for New Media Design at RIT is done in teams along with the New Media Developers. We are tasked with developing an interactive project and a matching exhibition to present the project to crowds at ImagineRIT. This year we are receiving guidance from a program and Y-Combinator alumnus to develop ideas and pitches that are ultimately fundable.

My team, Ambio, is going to use wearables to determine mood from health data, and then use that figure as a tool in fostering ambient connections and passive communication.

Shout out to the team - Conner Hasbrouck, Julianne Burke, Jessica Witley, Sudarshan Ashok, Vincent Lin, Josh Ladisic, and Bennoni Thomas
