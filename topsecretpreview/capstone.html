Our capstone project for New Media Design at RIT is done in teams along with the New Media Developers. We are tasked with developing an interactive project and a matching exhibition to present the project to crowds at ImagineRIT.

My team, Ambio, is going to use wearables to determine mood from health data, and then use that figure as a tool in fostering ambient connections and passive communication.
