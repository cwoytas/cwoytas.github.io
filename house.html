The intention of this project was to explore the possibilities of home comfort control. I was specifically intrigued with creating an interface that would allow users to confidently control the temperature of their home without needing to see the actual value. This spun into the idea of adjusting comfort relative to how you feel and changing the paradigm of thermostat control.
